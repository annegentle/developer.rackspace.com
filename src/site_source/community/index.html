Community & Outreach

Rackspace is committed to supporting developers and technologists. Our developer advocates write code and documentation and build tools to make the Rackspace Cloud Platform and OpenStack easy to use. We give talks, organize conferences, teach workshops, and field questions from people around the world. We also offer sponsorships for technical events and discounted hosting for OSS projects. Let us know what kind of support you need, and a Racker will reach out to you.

Open Source Contributions

Open source contributions are part of the job description for many Rackers. We build tools in-house and share with the community; we accept pull requests and we mentor and answer questions; and we design and implement new tools in the open. We also contribute to open source projects outside of the Rackspace and OpenStack ecosystems.

Event Sponsorship Program

In 2014, Rackspace developers and developer advocates attended, sponsored, spoke at, and facilitated hundreds of conferences, meetups, and workshops for developers and technologists around the world. This "fanatical support" in the field is central to Rackspace's mission: we want to meet developers where they are, in their communities, and show them the love and support they need to build amazing things on the Rackspace Cloud Platform. For more information about our Events Sponsorship Program, contact us at sponsorships@rackspace.com.

OSS Hosting Program

Since 2013, Rackspace has provided generous discounts on hosting for more than 150 OSS projects and communities. We support a wide range of projects from the security, scientific computing, big data, open hardware, and Python communities, among others. For more information about our OSS Hosting Program, contact us at sponsorships@rackspace.com.

What type of events does the Rackspace sponsor?

We sponsor large and small conferences, meetups, workshops, hackathons, and other kinds of tech community gatherings. We like to be hands-on with the communities we support, so we almost always attend the events we sponsor.

What kind of sponsorships do you provide?

Usually we offer financial support through our Events Sponsorship Program, but we can also provide space for meetups and workshops in our Austin, San Francisco, and Blacksburg offices. Let us know what you're looking for. We also offer discounted hosting for open source projects and open communities through our OSS Hosting Program.

Where is the Developer Experience team based?

Our developer advocates, developers, and information developers are located in the San Francisco, Seattle, Chicago, Detroit, DC, Austin, San Antonio, Columbus, Salt Lake City, New York, Philadelphia, and Pittsburgh metro areas. We also have developer advocates in London and Berlin.

Can I get a Racker to speak or teach at my event?

Yes! We love to attend and participate in the events we sponsor. If a Racker is available and in the area, we'll send her to give a talk at your event.
